Pickled Cucumbers

A refreshing and crunchy snack!

They're so yummy!

A pickled cucumber – commonly known as a pickle...
– is a usually small or miniature cucumber
that has been pickled in a brine, vinegar,
or other solution and left to ferment.
The fermentation process is executed either by
immersing the cucumbers in an acidic solution or
through souring by lacto-fermentation. Pickled
cucumbers are often part of mixed pickles.
